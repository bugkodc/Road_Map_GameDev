# 🎙️ Newsletters & Podcasts

Stay updated on software architecture trends and sharpen your engineering soft skills with weekly newsletters and industry podcasts.

---

### 1. ByteByteGo Newsletter
*   **Description:** A bi-weekly email newsletter that simplifies complex system design concepts and analyzes real-world system architectures (like Redis, Kafka, YouTube). Highly concise and visual, trusted by over 1 million subscribers.
*   **Link:** [bytebytego.com/newsletter](https://bytebytego.com)

---

### 2. Software Engineering Daily
*   **Description:** Daily, in-depth interviews with software engineers and architects covering architectural decisions, emerging technologies, and real-world system deployments.
*   **Link:** [softwareengineeringdaily.com](https://softwareengineeringdaily.com)

### 3. Soft Skills Engineering
*   **Description:** A weekly advice podcast where experienced software engineers answer non-technical questions about soft skills, developer career growth, communication, and team dynamics.
*   **Link:** [softskills.audio](https://softskills.audio)
